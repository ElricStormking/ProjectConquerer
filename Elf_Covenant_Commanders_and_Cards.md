The Verdant Covenant Commander & Card Design Pack (Markdown)  
This document contains all design details for The Verdant Covenant (Selesnya), including 3 commanders and 39 faction core deck cards.

# The Verdant Covenant – Commanders and Complete Deck  
Faction Identity: Creature construction, bioluminescent plants, life linkage, elite enhancement, community collaboration, resource recycling.

=======================================  
1. COMMANDERS  
=======================================  
## Commander 1: Warden of the Bloom: Liana (Liana – Warden of the Bloom)  
Role: Group buff / horizontal board expansion / creature cooperation  
Passive Skill – Universal Resonance: For each additional friendly unit on the field, all units gain 2% attack speed and movement speed (up to 20%). When a new unit is deployed, nearby allies receive a small amount of healing.  
Active Skill – Overgrowth: Cooldown 40s. Area attack that immediately summons 3 low-health melee units called "Nature Spirits" within the area, lasting 5 seconds.

## Commander 2: The Emerald Blade: Kaelas (Kaelas – The Emerald Blade)  
Role: Elite enhancement / guardian knight / single-target burst  
Passive Skill – Legendary Mark: At the start of each wave, automatically applies "Emerald Guard" to the unit with the highest Cost on the field: +30% max health, attacks deal additional damage equal to 2% of max health. Effect lasts 60 seconds or until the wave ends.  
Active Skill – Judgment of the World Tree: Cooldown 35s. Fires a powerful natural shockwave that knocks back nearby enemies; friendly units in the area gain 2 seconds of invincibility.

## Commander 3: The Soul-Seer: Malath (Malath – The Soul-Seer)  
Role: Life conversion / resource support / battlefield sustain  
Passive Skill – Vital Recirculation: Healing overflow on friendly units is converted into an equivalent shield. When a shield is generated, the next card deployment has a 10% chance to reduce its Cost by 1.  
Active Skill – Soul Bond: Cooldown 45s. Links the health of friendly units within an area for 6 seconds. Damage and healing received are evenly shared among linked units, and all gain 30% lifesteal.

2. UNIT CARDS - 21 Cards  
=======================================  
[Liana Specialized Units]  
## 1. Glow-Sprout Spirit – Cost 1  
Split Propagation: Low health and attack; upon death, spawns 2 immobile "Vine Tendril" soldier units at the location that attack enemies, lasting 20 seconds or until the wave ends.

## 2. Spore-Wing Scout – Cost 2  
Flying / Recon: Reduces hit rate of nearby enemy ranged units and increases movement speed of allies.

## 3. Verdant Legionary – Cost 2  
Cooperation: For each nearby Verdant Legionary soldier, increases own attack by 5%, up to 50%.

## 4. Root-Kin Swarm – Cost 3  
Basic small melee unit.

## 5. Seed-Pod Artillery – Cost 4  
Ranged AoE: Attacks deal small area damage; 20% chance to spawn a temporary "Poison Flower" soldier unit at the hit location, lasting 10 seconds, causing enemies in the area to take damage per second.

## 6. Pollen Burster – Cost 3  
Melee / Area Disruption: Each time attacked, sprays pollen around, reducing attack speed of enemy melee units by 15% for 2 seconds.

Tactical Role: Acts as frontline in swarm tactics, protecting fragile small units.

## 7. Bloom Thrower – Cost 2  
Ranged / Summon: On attack hit, 20% chance to spawn a "Poison Flower" soldier unit under the target, lasting 10 seconds, causing enemies in the area to take damage per second.

Tactical Role: Unit proliferation through offensive "war sustains war" strategy.

[Kaelas Specialized Units]  
## 8. Emerald Justiciar – Cost 5  
Elite: Gains 5% attack power for each buff owned (up to 30%).

## 9. Kaelas's Squire – Cost 2  
Guardian: Prioritizes protecting the nearby highest Cost unit, absorbing 20% of damage for it.

## 10. Guardian of the World Tree – Cost 6  
Heavy: Extremely high armor and knockback resistance; the strongest tank in Kaelas’s tactics.

## 11. Emerald Dragonling – Cost 4  
Flying: Breathes green flames, dealing extra damage to injured enemies.

## 12. Champion of the Glade – Cost 4  
Duelist: Gains 40% additional attack speed when fighting enemy elite units.

## 13. Emerald Vanguard – Cost 4  
Elite / Charge: First attack on entering combat charges toward the target. During charge, is in "super armor" state (immune to knockback), and hitting the target causes a small area stun.

Tactical Role: Clears the way for Kaelas’s elite troops, ensuring core damage dealers can engage smoothly.

## 14. Hallow-Tree Paladin – Cost 5  
Elite / Guardian: Gains 30% magic resistance when above 80% health. Automatically removes one negative status from a nearby ally every 15 seconds.

Tactical Role: Provides high-quality team protection; a stabilizer for single-target enhancement builds.

[Malath Specialized Units]  
## 15. Soul-Seer Disciple – Cost 2  
Healing Trigger: Each time healed, deals equal energy pulse damage to nearby enemies.

## 16. Spirit-Bound Deer – Cost 3  
Lifesteal: Basic attacks grant 15% lifesteal. Upon death, heals nearby allies.

## 17. Oracle – Cost 4  
Resource Cooperation: Increases all healing effects within its range by 15%.

## 18. Ethereal Weaver – Cost 3  
Support: Basic attacks do not deal damage but apply a stacking slow effect on the target.

## 19. Grove Petitioner – Cost 3  
Support: Heals the nearest injured ally.

## 20. Soul-Light Butterfly – Cost 1  
Flying / Support: No attack ability. Friendly units within its range gain 10% additional lifesteal, non-stacking.

Tactical Role: Low-cost buff source, strengthening Malath’s life sustain mechanism.

## 21. Vitality Bonder – Cost 3  
Ranged / Healing: Its normal attacks simultaneously heal all friendly units along the projectile path (amount equals 50% of damage dealt).

Tactical Role: Balances output and group healing, providing highly efficient feedback in Malath’s linkage tactics.

3. STRUCTURE CARDS - 9 Cards  
=======================================  
[Commander Specialized Structures]  
## 1. Bloom Hatchery – Cost 4 (Liana)  
Produces a "Glow-Sprout Spirit" every 8 seconds, maintaining unit number advantage.

## 2. Altar of Heroes – Cost 3 (Kaelas)  
Grants 20% damage reduction to the unit with the fewest soldiers within range.

## 3. Emerald Shield Battery – Cost 4 (Kaelas)  
At the start of each wave, provides damage-reducing magic shields to units in the area.

## 4. Soul-Stone Monument – Cost 3 (Malath)  
Collects souls of nearby fallen units; for every 3 collected, emits a small healing pulse to the entire field.

## 5. Spore-Mist Pillar – Cost 2 (Liana)  
Sprays smoke to conceal allies, granting 20% ranged evasion to units nearby.

## 6. Healing Grove – Cost 5 (Malath)  
Large area continuous healing zone; core of Malath’s defensive line.

[Faction Common Structures]  
## 7. Sun-Crystal Spire – Cost 5  
Turret: Fires highly accurate solar beams, with bonuses against flying units.

## 8. Living Vine Wall – Cost 1  
Defense: High health barrier; melee attackers receive 10% thorn damage.

## 9. Fountain of Life – Cost 3  
Resource/Healing: Slowly restores health of nearby units and structures.

4. MODULE CARDS - 9 Cards  
=======================================  
[Commander Specialized Modules]  
## 1. Swarm Protocol – Cost 2 (Liana)  
Attached to structures. When total soldiers of nearby units exceed 10, nearby units gain 15% additional damage.

## 2. Seed Dispersion – Cost 2 (Liana)  
Attached to units. On first attack hit, spawns a temporary Nature Spirit.

## 3. Undying Glory – Cost 3 (Kaelas)  
Attached to units. When receiving lethal damage, retains 1 health and becomes invincible for 2 seconds (once per wave).

## 4. Mark of Leadership – Cost 2 (Kaelas)  
Attached to units. Increases size by 15%, basic attacks gain area splash effect.

## 5. Life Energy Conversion Tank – Cost 2 (Malath)  
Attached to structures. Stores overflow healing and releases equivalent damage in one burst when enemies approach the structure.

## 6. Soul Linkage – Cost 2 (Malath)  
Attached to units. Damage taken by soldiers in this unit is shared among all soldiers within the unit.

[Faction Common Modules]  
## 7. Resilient Bark – Cost 2  
Defense: Increases max health by 25% and grants minor automatic health regeneration.

## 8. Emerald Mark – Cost 1  
Buff: Increases attack power by 10%. Deals 15% additional damage to "Structure" targets.

## 9. Photo-Core – Cost 2  
Resource: Attached to structures. At battle start, player immediately gains 1 temporary Cost point.
