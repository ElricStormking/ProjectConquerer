# Jade Dynasty Commander & Card Design Pack (Markdown)
This document contains all design details for the **Jade Dynasty faction**, including all **3 Commanders** and the **complete 34-card faction card set**.

# # **Jade Dynasty – Commanders & Full Card Set**
Faction Identity: Eastern Chi-Tech martial arts, monks, ninjas, spirit healers, Shikigami, talisman magic, chi-based crowd control, stuns, mobility.

---

# =======================================
# 1. **COMMANDERS**
# =======================================

# ## **Commander 1: Long Jin – Spear Saint**
**Role:** Chi Spearman / AoE Melee Control / Formation Breaker

### **Passive – Flowing Dragon Stance**
- Allies (melee) within 4 tiles gain:  
  - +10% Move Speed  
  - +15% Attack Speed  
  - +10% Chi Damage  
  - Melee crits apply **0.5s mini-stun** (ICD: 4s per unit)
- When Long Jin or melee units stun an enemy → **+1 Chi Flow**

---

### **Active Skill – Azure Stallion Charge**
**Cooldown:** 18s  
**Type:** Dash / AoE / Stun  

**Effect:**  
- Charge 8 tiles in target direction  
- Deals **200% ATK Chi Damage**  
- Stuns basic enemies **1.25s**, elites **0.75s**, bosses **0.3s stagger**  
- On landing:  
  - 360° **Chi Spear Sweep** (120% ATK)  
  - Applies **Chi Shock** (+15% damage taken for 4s)
- Leaves Chi fire trail for 3s (40 DPS + slow)
- If hitting 6+ enemies → **40% cooldown refund**

---

### **Secondary Passive – Dragon Tail Sweep**
(Unlockable via relics/meta progression)

Every 5th attack triggers:
- Cone AoE (120° arc)
- 80% ATK Chi Damage
- Knockback 1 tile
- **0.5s stun**

---

### **Playstyle:**  
Melee rush, stun chaining, AoE disruption, spear formations.

---

# ## **Commander 2: Hanami Reika – Spirit Miko Priestess**
**Role:** Healer / Support / Summoner / Control

### **Passive – Talisman of Purifying Winds**
Every 5s:
- Heals a random ally for **5% Max HP**
- Cleanses debuffs  
- **Generates 1 Chi Flow**

---

### **Active Skill – Twelve-Shikigami Ritual**
**Cooldown:** 24s  
**Type:** Area Summon / Control  

**Effect:**  
Summons **3 random Shikigami** for 10 seconds:

| Spirit | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| Fox Spirit | Fast ranged homing flames |
| Blue Oni | Bruiser melee |
| Paper Doll | Taunt tank |
| Spirit Lantern | Healing aura |
| Crow Familiar | -10% DEF debuff |

Allies in ritual circle gain **+30% heal received**.

---

### **Playstyle:**  
Healing, sustain, spirit summons, cleansing, supportive control.

---

# ## **Commander 3: Kasumi Nightwind – Master Ninja**
**Role:** Assassin / Teleport / Mirage / Crit Burst

### **Passive – Shadow Lotus Style**
- Ninjas gain:  
  - +12% Crit Chance  
  - +25% Backstab Damage  
- Commander: +20% dodge  
- Ninja crits → **+1 Chi Flow**

---

### **Active Skill – Shadowstep Backroll**
**Cooldown:** 14s  
**Type:** Teleport / Burst / Clone  

**Effect:**  
- Teleport up to 6 tiles  
- Execute **5-hit flurry** (each 40% ATK)  
- Auto-crit vs ranged/casters  
- Leaves **Shadow Clone** (8s):  
  - Taunts enemies 2.5s  
  - Explodes on death for AoE DMG  
- On kill:  
  - Cooldown -50%  
  - +3 Chi Flow  

---

### **Playstyle:**  
High mobility, assassination, clone disruption, crit stacking.
