# The Forbidden Bloodline Commander & Card Design Pack (Markdown)
This document contains all design details for **The Eternal Frost Clan - The Forbidden Bloodline faction**, including all **3 Commanders** and the **complete 34-card faction card set**.

---

# # **The Forbidden Bloodline – Commanders & Full Card Set**
Faction Identity: Vampire Nobility, Lich Ritualism, Live Torture, Forbidden Science, Blood, Decay, Soul Harvest.

---

# =======================================
# 1. **COMMANDERS**
# =======================================

# ## **Commander 1: Azariel – The Lich King**
**Role:** Soul Blasphemer / AoE Decay Control / Resource Converter

### **Passive – Master of Torment**
- Allies gain **+1 Essence of Torment** (50% chance) when nearby enemies take Damage over Time (DoT).
- Enemy healing is converted to **50% Decay Damage**.

---

### **Active Skill – Soul Blasphemy**
**Cooldown:** 20s
**Type:** AoE / Decay / Heal Reversal

**Effect:**
- Inflicts **Persistent Decay Damage** on a large target area for 8s.
- During decay, all affected enemy units' **healing received** is completely converted into **equal damage**.

---

# ## **Commander 2: Bellucci – The Blood Queen**
**Role:** Bloodthirsty Striker / Life Exchange / Quick Harvester

### **Passive – Eternal Thirst**
- Every time the Baron successfully **eliminates** an enemy, the **Max HP** of all nearby **friendly** units is **permanently reduced by 1%**.
- Baron gains **+25% Attack Speed** for 4s.

---

### **Active Skill – Sacrificial Feast**
**Cooldown:** 15s
**Type:** Single Target / Life Siphon / Self-Buff

**Effect:**
- Randomly targets **two nearby friendly units**, immediately siphoning **30%** of their **Current HP** to the Baron.
- Baron gains **+50% Attack Damage** for 6s.
- **Units** cannot be sacrificed to death by this effect (minimum 1 HP remains).

---

# ## **Commander 3: Zhaquille –  the Frankenstein**
**Role:** Live Experimentation / Shield Absorption / Battlefield Chaos Maker

### **Passive – Resonance of Pain**
- When a friendly **Abomination** unit is eliminated nearby, it has a **40% chance** to leave behind a **Flesh Remnant** structure.
- **Flesh Remnant** deals minor persistent damage to surrounding enemies every 2s.

---

### **Active Skill – Flesh Link**
**Cooldown:** 25s
**Type:** AoE / Shield / Damage Transfer

**Effect:**
- Links **all friendly units** in range for 4s.
- During this time, **50%** of **all damage** taken by friendly units is transferred to the Commander.
- Commander gains a **Temporary Shield** equal to **10% of the sum of all linked units' current HP**.

---

# =======================================
# 2. **UNIT CARDS - 13**
# =======================================

# ## **1. Flesh Weaver – Cost 1**
- Belong to Commander 3: Zhaquille –  the Frankenstein
- **Abomination / Melee**
- **Self-Sacrifice:** Deals AoE damage to **all nearby units** (including friendly) upon elimination.
- **Tactical Role:** Low-cost fodder, used for condition triggers and initial AoE threat.

---

# ## **2. Bound Spectre – Cost 2**
- Belong to Commander 2: Bellucci – The Blood Queen
- **Ethereal / Flying**
- **Curse Aura:** Permanently reduces the **Damage Output** of nearby enemy units by a **small amount**.
- **Tactical Role:** Aerial harassment, constantly weakens enemy attack power.

---

# ## **3. Bloodline Noble – Cost 3**
- Belong to Commander 2: Bellucci – The Blood Queen
- **Vampire / Mobile**
- **Blood Lash:** Attacks inflict **Bleeding** (Damage over Time) on the enemy.
- **Tactical Role:** High-mobility melee unit, excels at sustained damage and cleanup.

---

# ## **4. Abomination – Cost 5**
- Belong to Commander 3: Zhaquille –  the Frankenstein
- **Abomination / Heavy Armor**
- **Life Lock:** Upon deployment, **20%** of its **Max HP Cap** is evenly distributed to nearby friendly units (does not consume current HP).
- **Tactical Role:** Core heavy tank, gains high HP at the expense of allied Max HP.

---

# ## **5. Forbidden Scientist – Cost 4**
- Belong to Commander 2: Bellucci – The Blood Queen
- **Human / Support**
- **Live Experimentation:** Periodically **reduces** a nearby friendly unit's **Max HP Cap** to **increase** that unit's **Damage**.
- **Tactical Role:** High-risk, high-reward support unit.

---

# ## **6. Screaming Coffin – Cost 2**
- Belong to Commander 1: Azariel – The Lich King
- **Undead / Structure**
- **Fear Aura:** Nearby enemy attacks have a **low chance to miss**.
- **Tactical Role:** Basic defense and area control with a mental debuff.

---

# ## **7. Flesh Crawler – Cost 1**
- Belong to Commander 3: Zhaquille –  the Frankenstein
- **Abomination / Fast**
- Attacks have a **10% chance** to inflict **Corrupt** (reduces healing received) on the target.

---

# ## **8. Shade Servant – Cost 2**
- Belong to Commander 2: Bellucci – The Blood Queen
- **Vampire / Melee**
- **Enslavement:** **10% chance** to convert the eliminated enemy unit into a **Flesh Weaver** upon kill.

---

# ## **9. Putrid Archer – Cost 3**
- Belong to Commander 1: Azariel – The Lich King
- **Undead / Ranged**
- Arrows leave behind a Decaying Gas, **slowing** enemy movement speed in the area.

---

# ## **10. Cursed Walker – Cost 4**
- Belong to Commander 1: Azariel – The Lich King
- **Lich / Support**
- Possesses an **Undying Aura**, making nearby friendly Undead units **immune** to all Debuff effects.

---

# ## **11. Agony Screamer – Cost 3**
- Belong to Commander 1: Azariel – The Lich King
- **Ethereal / AoE**
- Each attack deals **additional** spiritual damage to enemies surrounding the target.

---

# ## **12. Eternal Watcher – Cost 5**
- Belong to Commander 2: Bellucci – The Blood Queen
- **Abomination / Giant**
- **Absorption:** Gains a small amount of **Temporary HP** whenever a unit is eliminated nearby.

---

# ## **13. Flesh Titan – Cost 6**
- Belong to Commander 3: Zhaquille –  the Frankenstein
- **Abomination / Core**
- **Rampage:** Attack Speed and Damage are significantly increased when HP is below 50%, but **constantly Self-Bleeds**.

---

# =======================================
# 3. **SPELL CARDS - 7**
# =======================================

# ## **1. Living Sacrifice – Cost 4**
- **Mode 3 (Conditional Trigger)**
- **Effect:** When **Essence of Torment** reaches max, automatically sacrifices a **low-cost unit** and immediately grants a one-time global buff (+10% Damage for all units, 8s).

---

# ## **2. Soul Shackles – Cost 3**
- **Mode 2 (Timed Interval Trigger)**
- **Effect:** Every so often, a dark chain symbol locks onto a random enemy unit, temporarily **reducing its armor**.

---

# ## **3. Pact of Blood – Cost 2**
- **Mode 3 (Conditional Trigger)**
- **Effect:** When a **high-cost unit** (>4 Cost) is eliminated, a **crimson blood pool** erupts at the location, healing nearby friendly units.

---

# ## **4. Plague Infestation – Cost 3**
- Inflicts a **vile plague** on the target area, dealing damage to all enemy units in the area every 1s for 6s.
- Units killed by the plague are converted into **Flesh Weavers**.

---

# ## **5. Forbidden Transmutation – Cost 5**
- Select a friendly **non-Undead** unit, immediately **transforming** it into an **Abomination**.
- The transformed unit gains a **Temporary Shield**.

---

# ## **6. Essence Burst – Cost 1**
- Instantly gain **5 Essence of Torment**.
- **Cost:** Sacrifices **10%** of the Current HP of all nearby **low-cost** friendly units.

---

# ## **7. Blood Rite – Cost 6**
- Eliminates all active **Flesh Weavers** on the field.
- Deals **100 Direct Damage** to the enemy Fortress for every **Flesh Weaver** eliminated.

---

# 4. **STRUCTURE CARDS - 7**
# =======================================

# ## **1. Living Altar – Cost 3**
- **Support**
- **Essence of Torment Production:** Periodically sacrifices a nearby friendly unit (random) to generate **3 Essence of Torment**.

---

# ## **2. Taboo Blood Pool – Cost 4**
- **Resource**
- **Essence Storage:** Generates **2 Essence of Torment** every so often.

---

# ## **3. Torment Spire – Cost 3**
- **Turret / Torment**
- **Decay Ray:** Attacks inflict **Decay** (persistent armor reduction) on the target.

---

# ## **4. Flesh Repair Device – Cost 2**
- **Support**
- **Sacrificial Repair:** Sacrifices a small amount of its own HP to heal a nearby injured unit.

---

# ## **5. Soul Harvester – Cost 4**
- **Structure / Resource**
- Has a **60% chance** to gain **1 Essence of Torment** whenever an enemy unit is eliminated nearby.

---

# ## **6. Abominable Hatchery – Cost 5**
- **Structure / Unit Production**
- Deploys a **Flesh Weaver** every 15s. Cooldown is **reduced by 3s** when a **friendly unit is eliminated** nearby.

---

# ## **7. Wall of Screams – Cost 2**
- **Defense / Wall**
- **Mental Strike:** Inflicts a **brief Fear** effect on the attacker (pauses attack for 0.5s) every time it is attacked.

---

# =======================================
# 5. **MODULE CARDS - 7**
# =======================================

# ## **1. Necrotic Weaving – Cost 2**
- **Support**
- **Deployment:** Deploys on a Fortress Tile, giving the Fortress a chance to transfer **50% of incoming damage** to a nearby friendly unit.

---

# ## **2. Eternal Servitude – Cost 3**
- **Buff**
- **Deployment:** Gives all **Vampire** and **Abomination** units' attacks a small chance to inflict **Mind Control** on the enemy, forcing them to briefly attack the **nearest unit** (friendly or foe).

---

# ## **3. Blood Enhancement – Cost 3**
- **Buff**
- **Deployment:** Increases the **Lifesteal/Siphon ability** of all nearby **Vampire** units by **100%**.

---

# ## **4. Agony Core – Cost 4**
- **Resource / Buff**
- **Deployment:** Has a **40% chance** to generate **1 Essence of Torment** whenever the Fortress takes damage.

---

# ## **5. Plague Infusion – Cost 2**
- **Buff**
- **Deployment:** Gives all **Undead** units' **attacks** a **20% chance** to inflict **Corrupt** on the target.

---

# ## **6. Forbidden Regeneration – Cost 5**
- **Support / Passive**
- **Deployment:** Grants **burst healing** whenever an **Abomination** unit's HP drops below **20%**.

---

# ## **7. Soul Pact – Cost 1**
- **Resource / Passive**
- **Deployment:** Instantly gain **3 Essence of Torment** whenever you **lose** a faction card slot (destroyed or transformed).
